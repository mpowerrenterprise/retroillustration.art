About us

The Origin of RETRO

Founded through focused design practice, RETRO has since developed into diverse creative worlds integrating illustration, animation, film and refined digital storytelling.

Our team of designers and storytellers combines creative design, branding strategy, digital marketing, and video production to bring stories to life. Every project is designed to connect with audiences, inspire action, and leave a lasting impression.

Our goal is to advance the way stories are created and experienced, guided by the same passion for design that shaped our beginnings. We approach every idea with thoughtful precision, ensuring clarity, beauty and purpose in the work we deliver. By collaborating closely with you, we transform vision into refined creative expression that endures, influences and supports meaningful connection.
